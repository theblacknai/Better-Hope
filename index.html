objectives

Education for us at BETTERHOPE is “knowledge and capacity for problem solving”. That is what BSC is geared to achieve. We are not just offering scholarship as it were, we have designed a system to ensure that the 1,000 beneficiaries of the scholarship graduate as bundles of solution to global issues. A breed of graduates that creates jobs or forms part of the life wire of the organization they work for. It is called – STREET TECHNOLOGY. A system that shows beneficiaries how to transfer knowledge into solution.
